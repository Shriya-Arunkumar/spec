    White noise

  • What is white noise?
    It's a sound that contains all audible sound frequencies in equal measure, creating a static-like sound

  • What does it help with?

    • Mask disruptive sounds: White noise is a type of sound masking that can help you sleep in noisy environments by blocking out other sounds.
    • Help you focus: White noise can help your brain stay focused by covering up distracting sounds. This can help you fall asleep faster and stay asleep longer.
    • Train your brain: White noise can help train your brain to know when it's time to sleep.
